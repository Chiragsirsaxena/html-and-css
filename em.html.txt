<h2>About</h2>
<p>Eoin Joseph Gerard Morgan CBE is an Irish-born cricketer who captains the England cricket team in limited overs cricket, Kolkata Knight Riders in the Indian Premier League and Middlesex in the Vitality t20 Blast</p>
